Camping.
Enjoy

Camping can be a nourishing, bonding experience with your friends, family, Mother Nature, or all three. In 2018, 41.67 million happy campers packed up their stuff and headed into the wilderness for a day or 2.

However, it can just as easily turn into a nightmare involving portable-stove-nuked sausages, collapsing tents, and invading bears from which you can’t really wake up. Luckily there are many steps you can take to nail camping (or tent peg it?) ahead of time and grab yourself a wholesome, outdoor experience.
